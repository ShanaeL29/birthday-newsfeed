Celebrating: Elijah Times 7!

Hot Spot: Kidzania

Kidzania is an entire indoor city run by kids! Elijah has such a huge imagination and there really is no limit to his future career dreams. From paleontoligist to chef, Elijah loves to explore the possibilities! So what better way to spend the day than testing out 100s of career options! The best part about it is that Elijah actually earned money for working and spent money to get a college degree and earn skills around the city...just like in real life!

First, Elijah and his godbrother, Jumeir, attended SMU for a 40 minute session earning degrees in Engineering and Education respectively. Obtaining their degrees allowed them to earn an additional income for each job they performed throughout the day! Next, the boys experienced what it was like to be veterinarians - where they practiced grooming animals, pilots - where they sat in an actual cockpit and completed a plane simulation from takeoff to landing, motion animators - where they created an entire animation clip piece by piece, construction workers, firefighters, optometrists, and even surgeons where they got to perform a kidney replacement operation on a dummy and revive their patient when he flatlined! There were so many opportunities and the boys loved each and every one! This place should most definitely be located in every state!
